
# Conclusion

As mentioned throughout this work, the Hijrī calendar is built upon one of the
greatest events in human history: the Hijrah of the Messenger of Allāh
\hon{\PBUH}{\BODY} from Makkah to Madīnah.

We have all come across the famous statement of the Prophet \hon{\PBUH}{\BODY}
to his beloved companion Abū Bakr al-Ṣiddīq \hon{\P_HIM}{\BODY}:

\HADITH_AR_START
لَا تَحْزَنْ إِنَّ اللَّهَ مَعَنَا
\HADITH_AR_END

\HADITH_START
“Do not grieve; indeed Allāh is with us.”
\HADITH_END

These are not merely words that look beautiful on a poster or a social media post.
They were spoken at one of the most difficult moments in the life of the Prophet
\hon{\PBUH}{\BODY}.

The Muslims were being persecuted for their faith. An assassination attempt had
just been carried out against the Messenger of Allāh \hon{\PBUH}{\BODY}. Armed
patrols were stationed around Makkah. A bounty of one hundred camels had been
placed upon the heads of the Prophet \hon{\PBUH}{\BODY} and Abū Bakr
\hon{\P_HIM}{\BODY}. Their provisions were limited. Abū Bakr \hon{\P_HIM}{\BODY}
had been stung in the Cave of Thawr. The enemy had tracked them to the very
entrance of the cave, only a few steps away. Yet it was at that very moment that
the Prophet \hon{\PBUH}{\BODY} calmly said, “Do not grieve; indeed Allāh is with
us.”

The strongest tribe in Arabia could not overcome two men because Allāh was their
Protector. These words are not merely a source of comfort; they are a promise
from Allāh to every believer who strives sincerely for His sake, places his
trust in Him, and remains steadfast upon His religion.

Allāh says,

\AYAH_AR_START
إِلَّا تَنصُرُوهُ فَقَدۡ نَصَرَهُ ٱللَّهُ إِذۡ أَخۡرَجَهُ ٱلَّذِينَ كَفَرُواْ ثَانِيَ ٱثۡنَيۡنِ إِذۡ هُمَا فِي ٱلۡغَارِ إِذۡ يَقُولُ لِصَٰحِبِهِۦ لَا تَحۡزَنۡ إِنَّ ٱللَّهَ مَعَنَاۖ فَأَنزَلَ ٱللَّهُ سَكِينَتَهُۥ عَلَيۡهِ وَأَيَّدَهُۥ بِجُنُودٖ لَّمۡ تَرَوۡهَا وَجَعَلَ كَلِمَةَ ٱلَّذِينَ كَفَرُواْ ٱلسُّفۡلَىٰۗ وَكَلِمَةُ ٱللَّهِ هِيَ ٱلۡعُلۡيَاۗ وَٱللَّهُ عَزِيزٌ حَكِيمٌ ٤٠
\AYAH_AR_END

\AYAH_START
If you do not aid him, Allāh has already aided him when those who disbelieved
drove him out as one of two, when they were in the cave and he said to his
companion, “Do not grieve; indeed Allāh is with us.” Then Allāh sent down His
tranquillity upon him, supported him with forces you could not see, and made the
word of those who disbelieved the lowest, while the Word of Allāh remained
supreme. And Allāh is All-Mighty, All-Wise. (Al-Tawbah, Q9:40)
\AYAH_END

Muḥarram also reminds us of another great victory granted by Allāh: the
salvation of Mūsá \hon{\S_HIM}{\BODY} and Bani Israel from Firʿawn. Surrounded
by fear, trapped between the sea and a tyrannical army, they witnessed the help
of Allāh arrive in a manner no one could have imagined.

The lesson remains the same. The people may change. The tyrants may change. The
trials may change. But Allāh never changes. The Lord who saved Mūsá
\hon{\S_HIM}{\BODY}, aided Muḥammad \hon{\PBUH}{\BODY}, strengthened al-Ḥusayn
\hon{\P_HIM}{\BODY}, and supported the believers throughout history is the same
Lord whom we worship today.

Let us therefore enter this blessed month with repentance, gratitude, worship,
fasting, charity, remembrance of Allāh, and renewed trust in Him. Let us honor
the Ahl al-Bayt and the Ṣaḥābah \hon{\P_THM}{\BODY} all together, as the
Messenger of Allāh \hon{\PBUH}{\BODY} taught us. Let us study our history with
honesty, authenticity, and balance. And let us hold firmly to the Quran and
Sunnah until we meet our Lord.

May Allāh accept this effort, forgive its shortcomings, multiply its benefit,
and make it a means of guidance for those who read it.

May Allāh elevate the mention of the family of His Messenger \hon{\PBUH}{\BODY},
be pleased with his noble Companions \hon{\P_THM}{\BODY}, unite the Ummah upon
the truth, and keep us firm upon the Sunnah until our final breath.

Imam al-Shāfiʿī \hon{\M_HIM}{\BODY} beautifully said:

::: {custom-style="Poetry Arabic" dir="rtl"}
يَا آلَ بَيْتِ رَسُولِ اللَّهِ حُبُّكُم *** فَرْضٌ مِنَ اللَّهِ فِي الْقُرْآنِ أَنْزَلَه
:::

::: {custom-style="Poetry Trans."}
| O Household of the Messenger, your love is God's decree,
|    A duty He revealed within the Qur'an for all to see.

O Household of the Messenger of Allāh! Loving you is an obligation that Allāh
revealed in the Quran.
:::


::: {custom-style="Poetry Arabic" dir="rtl"}
كَفَاكُمْ مِنْ عَظِيمِ الْقَدْرِ أَنَّكُم *** مَنْ لَمْ يُصَلِّ عَلَيْكُمْ فَلَا صَلَاةَ لَه
:::

::: {custom-style="Poetry Trans."}
| Your lofty rank and noble worth need no greater display:
|    Whoever sends no prayers on you has no true prayer that day.

As proof of your lofty rank, it is sufficient that the
one who does not invoke blessings upon you, his prayer is incomplete.
:::

May Allāh make us amongst those who truly love the Messenger of Allāh
\hon{\PBUH}{\BODY}, his family, his Companions, and all those who follow their
path with excellence until the Day of Judgment.
